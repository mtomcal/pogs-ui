About Us

The POGs 2.0 database provides an update of POGs version 1.0 (Walker et al. 2007) in the following ways:

  • Two species added: Populus trichocarpa, Zea mays.
  • Uses Gramene's ENSEMBL orthology prediction output rather than InParanoid
  • Adds orthology prediction comparison between different methods (e.g. Gramene and Plaza) to verify group consistency
  • Improved Phylogenetic tree accuracy with a revised algorithm
  • Includes up to date proteome data from PPDB and gene annotations imported from TAIR, Rice Annotation Project, MaizeSequence.org, and Phytozome.net
  • Improved User Experience via improved schema and indexing to promote rapid searches.
