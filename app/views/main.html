Description


POGs 2.0 Beta, is a relational database designed to facilitate cross-species inferences about gene functions and gene models in plants. The database integrates data from rice (Orza sativa), maize (Zea mays), Arabidopsis thaliana, and Poplar (Populus trichocarpa) by placing the complete predicted proteomes into "putative orthologous groups" (POGs). POGs were imported from Grameneâ€™s ENSEMBL orthology prediction output.

Each POG entry includes putative orthologs annotated with gene descriptions imported from species-specific databases, graphical representations of conserved protein domains, phylogenetic trees showing closely-related proteins, intracellular targeting predictions and verified targeting data from PPDB. The database can be queried by Interpro domain keyword or ID, genemodel or transcript accessions, known or predicted intracellular location, or any combination of the above.
